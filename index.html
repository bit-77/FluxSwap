◈ FAQ

Everything About
FluxSwap Answered

Your complete guide to swapping, staking, and earning FLUX on TRON with FluxSwap.

What is FluxSwap?+
FluxSwap is the premier decentralized exchange (DEX) and staking protocol built specifically for FLUX token on the TRON blockchain. It aggregates 400+ TRON liquidity pools to find the best FLUX swap rates, offers staking tiers up to 280% APY, and enables cross-chain FLUX swaps across 30+ networks — all in one non-custodial, zero-KYC interface.
What is FLUX token on TRON?+
FLUX on TRON (TRC-20 contract: TWr6yzukRwZ53HDe3bzcC8RCTbiKa4Zzb6) is a parallel asset of the native Flux blockchain. The Flux ecosystem uses parallel assets — token bridges that allow FLUX to exist simultaneously on multiple blockchains while remaining pegged to the same supply. FLUX-TRON gives holders access to TRON's ultra-fast, ultra-cheap DeFi ecosystem without leaving the Flux ecosystem. There will only ever be 440 million FLUX in total supply across all chains.
How do I swap USDT to FLUX on FluxSwap?+
Click "Connect Wallet" and connect TronLink. Select USDT (TRC-20) as your input token and FLUX as your output token. Enter the amount of USDT to spend. Review the live FLUX quote, exchange rate, route, and fee breakdown. Set slippage tolerance (0.5% recommended for FLUX). Click "Swap FLUX Now" and confirm the transaction in TronLink. Your FLUX tokens arrive in your wallet in under 3 seconds thanks to TRON's fast finality.
What is FLUX staking on FluxSwap and how does it work?+
FLUX staking on FluxSwap lets you deposit FLUX tokens into the protocol smart contract to earn passive yield. Choose Flexible staking (no lock, 42% APY) or Fixed staking (lock 30–365 days, up to 280% APY). Rewards accrue daily and are distributed in FLUX tokens directly to your staking position. Fixed stakers also receive loyalty multipliers and tier bonuses. The staking contract is audited and non-custodial — only your wallet address can ever withdraw your staked FLUX.
What APY can I earn staking FLUX?+
FluxSwap offers tiered APY: Flexible staking earns 42% APY with no lock. Fixed 30-day staking earns 72% APY. Fixed 90-day lock earns 128% APY. Fixed 180-day lock earns 196% APY. The maximum 280% APY is available for the 365-day fixed lock. Flux Node operators who stake receive an additional multiplier up to 3.2x on their base APY. All rates compound daily and are paid in FLUX.
What wallets work with FluxSwap?+
FluxSwap supports TronLink (recommended — official TRON wallet), TokenPocket, Trust Wallet, BitKeep/Bitget Wallet, imToken, and any WalletConnect-compatible wallet that supports TRON and TRC-20 tokens. For cross-chain swaps from Ethereum or BNB Chain, MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, and all EVM-compatible wallets are fully supported. Zelcore wallet (the official Flux wallet) is also compatible with FluxSwap for TRON operations.
What are the fees on FluxSwap?+
FluxSwap charges a protocol fee starting at 0.01% of the swap value. TRON network fees are minimal — typically $0.007–$0.015 per transaction. There are no account fees, withdrawal fees, or staking entry fees. Node Runner tier stakers receive a 50% fee rebate on all their FluxSwap swaps. All fees are fully disclosed in your quote before you confirm — no hidden costs, ever.
How does FluxSwap find the best FLUX rate?+
FluxSwap's routing engine simultaneously queries 400+ TRON DEX pools including SunSwap, JustSwap, SunStable, and all FLUX-paired liquidity sources. A pathfinding algorithm optimally splits your order across multiple pools to reduce price impact and maximize FLUX output. Quotes refresh every 5 seconds for accuracy. For cross-chain FLUX swaps, bridge costs and destination chain fees are factored into the net rate so you always see the true best deal.
Is FluxSwap safe? Are the contracts audited?+
Yes. FluxSwap is fully non-custodial — your FLUX is never held by the protocol between transactions. All smart contracts, including the swap router and staking contracts, are audited by leading blockchain security firms. MEV protection shields every swap from front-running bots. The staking contracts are time-locked and cannot be modified after deployment. If a swap cannot complete for any reason, your tokens are automatically returned to your wallet — no funds can ever be lost in a failed transaction.
What is MEV protection and how does FluxSwap implement it?+
MEV (Maximal Extractable Value) bots monitor public mempools and front-run pending swaps, forcing traders into worse prices. FluxSwap routes all FLUX swap transactions through private TRON relay networks, making your swap invisible to bots before it confirms on-chain. This ensures you always receive the exact quoted rate. For large FLUX swaps, MEV protection can save traders $20–$100+ per transaction compared to unprotected DEXes.
Can I swap FLUX cross-chain from Ethereum or other networks?+
Yes. FluxSwap supports cross-chain FLUX swaps from Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, Solana, Avalanche, Arbitrum, Optimism, zkSync, and more. You can bridge and swap in one step — send ETH from MetaMask and receive FLUX TRC-20 in TronLink, for example. FluxSwap also integrates with the native Flux parallel asset bridge, allowing movement between the native Flux chain and TRON seamlessly. Cross-chain swaps typically complete in 1–5 minutes depending on the source chain.
What are FluxSwap staking tiers?+
FluxSwap has four tiers: Flux Starter (1–9,999 FLUX staked) earns base APY. Flux Holder (10,000–49,999 FLUX) earns base APY + 30% bonus and a 20% fee rebate. Flux Validator (50,000–199,999 FLUX) earns base APY + 70% bonus, a 35% fee rebate, and priority routing on swaps. Flux Node Runner (200,000+ FLUX) earns maximum APY + 3.2x multiplier, a 50% fee rebate, and exclusive early access to new FLUX token listings and governance votes.
What is slippage and how should I set it for FLUX?+
Slippage is the acceptable price difference between your quoted rate and execution price, caused by market movements between quote and confirmation. For most FLUX/USDT swaps, 0.5% slippage is ideal. For large trades (over $10,000 in FLUX) or periods of high volatility, 1% is recommended. The "Auto" setting on FluxSwap dynamically selects the optimal slippage based on FLUX pool depth and recent volatility data, and is the best choice for most users.
Can I provide liquidity to FLUX pools on FluxSwap?+
Yes. FluxSwap allows LPs to deposit FLUX paired with USDT TRC-20 into the primary FLUX/USDT liquidity pool. In return you receive LP tokens representing your pool share and earn a proportional percentage of all swap fees generated by that pair. LP positions are additionally eligible for FLUX yield farming incentives on top of trading fees, making liquidity provision one of the highest-yield strategies for long-term FLUX believers.
Do I need KYC or an account to use FluxSwap?+
No. FluxSwap is a fully permissionless protocol. There are no accounts, no registrations, no email addresses, and absolutely no KYC verification of any kind. Your wallet address is your identity on-chain. Connect TronLink and you can instantly swap FLUX, stake for rewards, and provide liquidity — with complete financial privacy and sovereignty over your assets.
How fast are FLUX swaps on TRON?+
FLUX swaps on TRON finalize in under 3 seconds. TRON's Delegated Proof-of-Stake consensus enables extremely high throughput — roughly 2,000 TPS — with near-instant finality. This makes TRON the fastest major blockchain for FLUX swaps, dramatically faster than Ethereum (12+ seconds), BNB Chain (3–5 seconds), or Polygon (2–4 seconds). The moment you confirm in TronLink, your FLUX appears in your wallet before you can look away.
What happens to my FLUX if a swap fails?+
If a FLUX swap fails — due to slippage exceeding tolerance, insufficient liquidity, or network conditions — the transaction reverts atomically. Your FLUX and USDT are returned to your wallet in full. FluxSwap's atomic swap architecture guarantees you can never be stuck mid-swap. On TRON, a failed transaction consumes a small amount of energy/bandwidth but zero FLUX is ever deducted from a reverted swap. To minimize failures: use appropriate slippage and ensure your wallet has sufficient TRX for network fees.
How do I unstake my FLUX from FluxSwap?+
For Flexible staking, click "Unstake" in the Staking dashboard at any time. Your FLUX and all accrued rewards return to your wallet immediately with no penalty. For Fixed staking, your position unlocks automatically once the lock period expires. After expiry, click "Withdraw" to retrieve your FLUX plus all earned rewards. Early withdrawal from a Fixed lock forfeits rewards for the remaining lock period, so only lock what you're comfortable holding for the chosen duration.
Does FluxSwap support the native Flux blockchain?+
Yes. FluxSwap integrates with the Flux parallel asset bridge, enabling movement between the native Flux blockchain and TRON. This means you can bridge native FLUX from Zelcore to TRON's TRC-20 FLUX, swap it on FluxSwap for USDT or other tokens, and bridge back — all without using a centralized exchange. FluxSwap is the primary DeFi gateway for Flux ecosystem participants who want to access TRON's deep liquidity and low-fee environment.
Does FluxSwap have a referral program?+
Yes. FluxSwap's referral program rewards you 20% of the protocol fee from every swap made by users you refer — paid in FLUX directly to your wallet in real time. There is no cap on referrals or earnings. Node Runner tier stakers earn a boosted 35% referral commission. Your referral dashboard tracks total referred wallets, their combined swap volume, and your accumulated FLUX referral rewards — all claimable at any time with zero restrictions.
